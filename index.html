Size Guide

Measurements in inches. This is a general guide — actual measurements can vary slightly by style.

How to measure yourself:
  • Chest/Bust — wrap a tape around the fullest part of your chest, under the arms.
  • Length — measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to where you want the hem.
  • Shoulder — measure across the back from one shoulder edge to the other.

Between two sizes? Check the fabric — cotton-linen has a little natural give, so many people find true-to-size works best.
